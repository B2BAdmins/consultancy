Administrative Solutions for Workforce Procurement

B2BAdmin connects companies with verified recruitment agencies through structured administrative processes. We do not recruit. We facilitate.

What We Do

Companies need skilled employees. Recruitment agencies have access to talent. B2BAdmin sits between them — handling the administrative coordination, vetting, and process management so both parties can focus on their core operations.

We maintain a curated network of recruitment agencies across multiple regions and industries. When a company submits a workforce requirement, we identify the appropriate agencies, coordinate the engagement, and oversee the administrative framework until the requirement is fulfilled.

Our Services

Administrative functions that bridge companies and recruitment agencies.

Agency Matching

We analyze your workforce requirements and match you with recruitment agencies in our network that specialize in your industry and region.

Contract Administration

We draft, review, and manage contractual agreements between your company and the recruitment agencies, ensuring terms are clear and enforceable.

Process Oversight

We monitor the engagement timeline, track deliverables, and ensure both parties adhere to agreed milestones and service levels.

Agency Vetting

All agencies in our network undergo verification of credentials, past performance review, and compliance checks before engagement.

Payment Coordination

We handle invoicing structures and payment scheduling between your company and the recruitment agencies to ensure smooth financial transactions.

Reporting & Compliance

Regular status reports on active engagements, along with documentation ensuring regulatory and internal compliance standards are met.

How It Works

A clear administrative workflow from requirement to fulfillment.

01

Requirement Intake

Your company submits detailed workforce requirements, including role specifications, volume, timeline, and location.

02

Agency Selection

We identify and shortlist recruitment agencies from our network that align with your specific needs and geographic scope.

03

Engagement Setup

We establish the administrative framework — contracts, SLAs, reporting schedules, and payment terms between all parties.

04

Oversight & Closure

We monitor the engagement, ensure deliverables are met, manage documentation, and close the administrative loop upon fulfillment.
