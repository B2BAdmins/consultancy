The Process

Four Steps.
One Contact.

From initial briefing to ongoing management — B2BAdmin handles the full administrative lifecycle. Your team has one point of contact throughout.

01
Initial Briefing
Your company submits its requirement to B2BAdmin. We document scope, budget, timeline, and operational context with full precision.
02
Needs Assessment
Our team analyses the brief and maps the right recruitment profile — sector, scale, urgency, and compliance all factored in.
03
Agency Connection
B2BAdmin introduces the matched partner agency, structures the engagement, and administers the formal handover with defined deliverables.
04
Ongoing Administration
Throughout the partnership B2BAdmin remains the administrative authority — managing communications, resolving issues, and tracking outcomes.
