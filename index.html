What We Do

Companies need proper documentation to employ international workers legally. B2BAdmin prepares, reviews, and files the required paperwork — employment contracts, work permit applications, compliance reports, and regulatory filings.

We do not find employees. We do not find employers. We do not connect parties. We ensure the documents between existing parties are correct, complete, and compliant.

Our Services

Administrative documentation for companies with international workforce needs.

Document Preparation

Drafting employment contracts, work permit applications, and regulatory filings according to EU, local, and international legal requirements.

Contract Review

Reviewing existing agreements between companies and recruitment agencies for administrative completeness and regulatory compliance.

Compliance Filing

Submitting required documentation to immigration authorities, labor inspectorates, and tax offices on behalf of our clients.

Deadline Tracking

Monitoring document expiration dates, renewal deadlines, and regulatory filing schedules to prevent penalties.

Translation & Notarization

Certified translations and notarization of documents in multiple languages including EU and worldwide standards.

Archive Management

Organized digital and physical document storage for audit readiness and legal reference.

How It Works
